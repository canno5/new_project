A web developer is a tech professional who designs, builds, and maintains websites and web applications. They bridge the gap between creative concepts and functional digital experiences, ensuring sites are visually appealing, easy to navigate, and perform efficiently across various devices.


An app developer (or application developer) is a specialized software engineer who designs, builds, and maintains programs for specific platforms—most commonly mobile devices, but also desktops and web browsers. Unlike general software developers, app developers focus heavily on the user-facing experience and optimizing software for specific hardware constraints like screen size, touch input, and sensors.
